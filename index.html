Welcome to Redfall FC

Founded in Waterfall by local residents, Redfall FC is more than a football club — it’s a community where friendships are built and passion for the game thrives.

Our Journey

Since 2017/2018, Redfall FC has grown from a small group of residents into a vibrant social football club. Over the years, we’ve gained and lost players, but our spirit has remained strong — united by the love of football and the pride of Waterfall.

We believe in teamwork, respect, and community. Whether on the pitch or off it, Redfall FC is about bringing people together.
