About Redfall FC

Founded in Waterfall by residents in 2017, Redfall FC is a social football club built on passion, pride, and friendship.

Our Story

From humble beginnings in 2017/2018, Redfall FC has grown into a welcoming community club. Players have come and gone, but the heart of the team remains — a family united by football and the pride of Waterfall.

Our Values

Community

We bring people together on and off the pitch.

Respect

We play fair and support one another.

Passion

We celebrate the beautiful game with pride.

Friendship

We build bonds that last beyond match day.

A Social Club

Redfall FC is more than competition — it’s about connection. We welcome players of all skill levels, supporters, and sponsors. Whether you’re here to play, cheer, or simply belong, Redfall FC has a place for you.
